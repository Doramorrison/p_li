GP PBL

Project-Based Learning (PBL) in a global perspective class immerses students in real-world issues like climate change, cultural diversity, and international relations. Through research and collaboration, students analyze global challenges and propose solutions, fostering critical thinking and cross-cultural understanding. This hands-on approach equips students with the skills and empathy needed to address complex global issues and become responsible global citizens.
